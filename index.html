Odin Menu

  • Jollof Rice

    A classic West African dish, Jollof rice is a one-pot meal featuring rice cooked in a tomato, onion, and bell pepper base. It's often spiced with thyme, curry powder, and sometimes scotch bonnet peppers for heat.

  • Pounded Yam and Egusi Soup

    Pounded yam is a smooth dough-like meal made from yam flour and usually served with Egusi soup. Egusi soup is made from melon seeds and typically includes leafy vegetables, palm oil, meat (like goat, beef, or fish), and seasonings.

  • Pepper Soup

    A light, spicy soup commonly made with goat meat, fish, or chicken. It's flavored with a variety of spices and herbs, including the unique 'pepper soup spice' mix, and is often enjoyed as a comfort food during colder seasons.

  • Moi Moi

    A steamed bean pudding made from ground, peeled beans, onions, and peppers. It can be enriched with sardines, corned beef, or boiled eggs. Moi Moi is often served as a side dish with rice or as a main meal with bread or pap.

  • Suya

    A popular street food, Suya is skewered and grilled meat (often beef, chicken, or ram) coated in a mix of spices including ground peanuts, paprika, and ginger. It's usually served with sliced onions, tomatoes, and cabbage.
